**Source:** YouTube video by Jay (LeadGenJ)  
**Duration:** 26:32  
**Link:** https://youtu.be/26YGlf4IxQE

## Core Concept: Reverse Lead Magnet

Instead of giving leads a PDF/ebook they must read and implement themselves, build them a **working micro-app** that solves an immediate problem. Value flows from you to them already implemented.

## Key Stats
- **~4% reply rate** on cold emails using this method
- **~1 hour per client vertical** to build the micro-app
- **Build once, use for all leads** in that vertical

## The Cold Email Sequence

1. **Email 1:** "Hey [name], I built a tool that creates custom [thing] for your [stack]. Would it be okay if I spent some time and put together a few automations you can import in one click? Just say yes."
2. **Email 2:** "Here you go" — send link to app with their URL pre-filled
3. **Follow-up:** Book a call

## Tools
- **Claude Code** (primary, most powerful)
- **Bolt.new / Lovable** (beginner-friendly, browser-based)
- **Vercel + Supabase** (hosting + backend)

## Who This Works For
- Targeting decision makers at SMBs (10-200 employees)
- Agencies selling to other agencies
- AI companies selling AI services
- Niches with tangible problems or "shiny object syndrome"
