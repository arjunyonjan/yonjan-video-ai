Key Insights

From the transcript analysis:

  • Reverse Lead Magnet: Instead of giving leads a PDF they must read, give them a working micro-app that solves an immediate problem.
  • Micro-SaaS Strategy: Build tiny purpose-built web apps per client vertical (SEO agencies, AI automation, etc.) in ~1 hour using Claude Code.
  • Cold Email Sequence: Email 1: "Should I build this for you?" → Email 2: "Here you go" with app link → Email 3: Follow-up.
  • Tools: Claude Code (primary), Bolt.new / Lovable (beginner-friendly), Vercel + Supabase for hosting.
  • Reply Rate: ~4% average reply rate, significantly higher than traditional cold email.
